CLIMATE CHANGE CHALLENGE



GAME RULES:

- The levels explains how much time you wil get to make a desccion for making your choice
- Drag and drop your choice in the choosen field between good, mediocre and bad
- There is 15 picture displayed in each level
- The picture is always randomized
- Once the timer runs out you will get a option to go to the next page for your scoring points
- The score is based on how good you preformed and how right you have placed the images

Once you have seen your score you could review you work of art and learn from the mistakes you have made.
